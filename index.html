WHAT IS THE ‘THINK OF IT’ – YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM

TOI-YLP attempts to mirror every aspect to you: directing and leading you to the root, motivating you to work upon a quick response to urgent problems.
YLP exists for the youth to act in unison with an aim to create an impact by addressing social, political, and environmental issues. It makes them; the youth realize the significance of a youth-led nation.
In very straightforward terms, ‘Think of It’ i.e. the ongoing issues pertaining to multifarious spheres, morality, ideals, the sense of justice that prevails and to conjecture upon ideas that should be prevailing instead. Youth and leadership share an intrinsic yet undervalued relationship, we are here to bridge the gap between the two and provide a platform to equip the leaders of tomorrow.
